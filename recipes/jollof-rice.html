Jollof Rice Recipes

Description

This is a recipes for making this delicious Nigeria delicacy popularly known as Nigeria Jollof, from complete beginner to pro chef or cook.

Ingredients

  • Two cup of Rice
  • One sachaet of Vegetable oil
  • Butter
  • Seasonings
  • 7 pieces of Pepper
  • 4 pieces of Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • one small can of Green beans
  • Tomato paste
  • Ketch up
  • Bay leave

Steps

  1. Grind your Pepper, Tomatoes, and onions together to turn into paste.
  2. Light up your cooking gas cylinder and put your cooking pot on the burner.
  3. Pour the vegetable oil and let it hot for about two minutes.
  4. Add your paste to the hot vegetable oil to fry and stir for like 3 minutes.
  5. Add some seasoning to your fried paste to make it delicious to your taste.
  6. Then leave it for like 3 minutes again.
  7. After three minutes, add a bowl of water to the fried paste.
  8. Then wash your rice twice and pour the rice into your cooking pot that has the paste and let it cook for like 10 minutes.
  9. Then your jollof rice is ready to be served.
