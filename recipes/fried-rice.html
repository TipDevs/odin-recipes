Fried Rice Recipes

Description

This is a recipe that provide step by step guide on how you can make this Nigeria delicacy known as Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Sparrow
  • Sweet corn
  • Liver
  • Onion Leave
  • Bell Pepper
  • Seasonings
  • Red Pea
  • Boiled beef/chicken water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Green Pepper
  • Bay leave
  1. Light up your cooking gas cylinder and put your cooking pot on the burner.
  2. Pour your vegetable oil into your cooking pot and add curry and thyme and stir for 1 minutes.
  3. Slice the liver, bell pepper, onion leaves, carrots into pieces.
  4. Then add all the slice ingredients to the vegetable oil and stir for like 5 minutes
  5. Then you remove everything from the pot and add the boiled beef/chicken water that can cook the rice till done into the pot.
  6. Wash the rice twice and pour the rice in the water inside the pot.
  7. Then leave the rice to cook for like 20 minutes then add all the fried sliced ingredients to the rice and stir till it mix and blend with the rice.
